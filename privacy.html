Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 02, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Animal Face Test ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using Animal Face Test, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the site.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Contact us through our contact form
  • Send us an email
  • Provide feedback or suggestions

This information may include your name, email address, and the content of your message.

Photo Data - Important Information

We do not collect, store, transmit, or access any photos you upload to use the Animal Face Test. Here's why:

  • All image processing occurs locally in your web browser using JavaScript
  • Your photos never leave your device
  • We do not have servers that receive or store image data
  • We cannot see, access, or analyze your photos
  • Once you close or refresh the page, the image data is discarded

This client-side processing approach was intentionally designed to ensure your complete privacy when using our facial analysis tool.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Pages viewed
  • Time spent on pages
  • General location information (country/region level, not precise location)

This information is collected through standard web server logs and analytics tools to help us understand how visitors use our site and improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our website and services
  • To respond to your inquiries and fulfill your requests
  • To send you technical notices and support messages
  • To understand how users interact with our website
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our site and services. These cookies collect information anonymously.

Advertising Cookies

Our website displays advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. These cookies allow Google to display relevant advertisements to you.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept first-party cookies only
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking cookies may affect your experience on our website and other sites.

This website uses Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the Internet.

Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Our security measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all website communications

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Animal Face Test is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
