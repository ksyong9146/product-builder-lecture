Which Animal Do You Look Like?

Our AI analyzes your facial features to reveal your inner animal look-alike. Are you more of a loyal, friendly dog or a graceful, independent cat? Upload a photo to find out!

How It Works: The Magic of AI

Our Animal Face Test uses a sophisticated artificial intelligence model called a "Convolutional Neural Network," which has been trained using Google's Teachable Machine platform. This model has analyzed thousands of images of dogs and cats to learn the subtle patterns and features that distinguish them, such as ear shape, eye spacing, and snout length. When you upload your picture, the AI compares your features to these learned patterns to calculate a probability.
